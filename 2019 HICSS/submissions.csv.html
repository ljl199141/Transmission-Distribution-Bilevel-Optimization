Table Help

⨯
  • To sort columns: Click on the column header. Click again to reverse the sort direction. For a sub-sort (when items in the existing sort column are equal) shift-click on another column header. Multiple sub-sorts are possible.
  • To show or hide columns: If the "Columns" button is visible, click it. Then choose which columns are to be visible.
  • To show or hide a RANGE OF COLUMNS: If the "Columns" button is visible, click it. Then click on one column to change its visibility. Then SHIFT-click on another column. All columns between the first-clicked column and the second-clicked column will change visibility to that of the first-clicked column.
  • To rearrange columns: Drag a column header left or right.
  • To search: If the "Search" box is visible, type a word or words into the box. Only those rows containing all of the the words (not necessarily in sequence) will be shown. To search for a multi-word phrase, enclose the phrase in double quotation marks. The "Search" box is highlighted to remind you that not all rows are being shown.
  • To show the table by pages: If the "Show" menu is visible, choose the number of rows to show on one page. For large tables, showing only part of the table will make rendering and searching much faster.
